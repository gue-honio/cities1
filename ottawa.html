Ottawa

Ottawa’s metropolitan area spans the Ottawa River, including Ottawa on the Ontario side and Gatineau on the Quebec side of the river. The climate is continental, with warm, humid summers and cold, usually snowy winters.

Temperatures average in the mid-teens F (about 10 °C) in January and in the low 70s F (about 21 °C) in July.
